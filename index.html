ALIGN Sports – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 11, 2026

ALIGN Sports is operated by ALIGN Apps. Contact: info@alignapps.com.

Information We Collect

Name, email address, team affiliation, user role, and optional profile photos necessary for app functionality.

Camera Access

The app requests camera access solely so users can take photos for the team gallery or set a profile photo. The camera is accessed only when actively used. No background recording, biometric analysis, tracking, or advertising use occurs.

Photo Storage & Processing

Photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage for sharing with authorized team members. No additional image processing occurs beyond storage and display.

Metadata

The app does not intentionally collect or use photo metadata (such as geolocation/EXIF data) for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

Push Notifications

Optional reminders and announcements. Notifications can be disabled in device settings.

Analytics

Limited diagnostic and usage data may be collected to improve performance. Not sold or used for advertising.

User Consent & Revocation

Permissions require explicit approval and may be revoked at any time via device settings. Core app functionality remains available without camera access except for photo features.

Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained while accounts remain active. Users may request deletion at info@alignapps.com.

Children Under 13

Accounts for users under 13 must be managed by a parent, legal guardian, or authorized coach with verifiable parental consent.

Security

Encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and reasonable safeguards are implemented.
